About This Website

Why should I use this website?

While many platforms rely solely on so-called "artificial intelligence" technology to boost their job postings, we take an old-school approach to tackle the digital noise and spam issue in today's job search process: curating jobs manually and providing AI-enhanced insights to enhance your search experience. Here, the term "AI" means love (愛) in Japanese, not some soulless machine learning algorithm.

How often are new jobs added?

We update our listings regularly to ensure you have access to the latest hand-picked opportunities. Bookmark us or sign up for alerts to stay in the loop! New features are actively developed at the moment.

How do I apply for jobs on your site?

Simply browse our listings, click on a job that interests you, and follow the instructions to apply. Most roles should link directly to the employer's website.

Is this website free to use?

Yes! Searching for jobs and applying through our site is completely free for job seekers. We’re here to connect you with the right opportunities—no strings attached.
