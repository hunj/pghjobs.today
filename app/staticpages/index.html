What is this website?

While searching for my next job, I got fed up with fake job listings, spammy job boards, and endless scrolling.
I built this website to help others in Pittsburgh find real job opportunities without that hassle.

How do I apply for jobs shown on this website?

Simply browse our listings, click on a job that interests you, and follow the instructions to apply. They should link directly to the employer's website.

Is this website free to use?

ABSOLUTELY YES. Job seekers should NOT be paying money to find a job. Although I can't provide any free job training or anything like that, searching for job openings on this website is completely free.
